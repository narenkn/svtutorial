Introduction

System Verilogas a language extends the Veriloglanguage and is a superset. Hence Veriloglanguage is in its entirety should be supported by System Verilogunless explicitly stated. The focus of the book would be to teach System Verilogand the assumption is that the reader is adequately knowledgeable in Verilog. There exists adequate texts for Verilog.
